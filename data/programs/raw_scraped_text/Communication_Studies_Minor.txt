Communication Studies Minor
Communication Studies Minor
Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program. 
Declaration Requirements
Before declaring this academic plan, see invalid credential combinations.
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative minor average of 65.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 4.0 units.
Course Requirements
Required Courses
keyboard_arrow_up
Complete all of the following
Complete 4 of the following: 
BLKST224 - Black Performance Studies (0.50)
COMMST100 - Interpersonal Communication (0.50)
COMMST101 - Theories of Communication (0.50)
COMMST220 - Performance Studies (0.50)
COMMST223 - Public Speaking (0.50)
COMMST224 - Black Performance Studies (0.50)
COMMST226 - Introduction to Race, Culture, and Communication (0.50)
COMMST228 - Public Communication (0.50)
THPERF220 - Performance Studies (0.50)
THPERF224 - Black Performance Studies (0.50)
Complete 0.5 unit at the 400-level from the following subject codes: COMMST, DAC
Complete 1.5 additional units at any level from the following subject codes: COMMST, DAC
Additional Constraints
Students may only complete one course from any cross-listed set.
Students may use one of COMMST220/THPERF220 or BLKST224/COMMST224/THPERF224 towards the first set of required courses listed above.
