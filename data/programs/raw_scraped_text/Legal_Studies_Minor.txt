Legal Studies Minor
Legal Studies Minor
Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program. 
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative minor average of 65.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 4.0 units.
Course Requirements
Required Courses
keyboard_arrow_up
Complete all of the following
Complete all the following: 
LS101 - Introduction to Legal Studies (0.50)
Complete 1 of the following: 
SOC101 - Introduction to Sociology (0.50)
SOC101R - Introduction to Sociology (0.50)
Complete 1.5 units of LS courses at the 200-level
Complete 1.5 units of LS courses at the 300-level or above
Additional Constraints
Students may only complete one course from any cross-listed set.
A minimum of 2.0 units taken to fulfil the requirements of this academic plan must be unique to the plan, meaning they cannot be used to satisfy the requirements of any other academic plan (major, minor, etc.).
