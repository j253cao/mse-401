Computer Science Minor
Computer Science Minor
Declaration Requirements
This credential is open to all Honours students and is generally not available to students outside the Faculty of Mathematics because it includes several restricted courses. Students inside the Faculty of Mathematics may need to register as a Computer Science major for one or more terms to complete the Computer Science Minor.
Before declaring this academic plan, see invalid credential combinations.
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative Computer Science average of 60.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 4.75 units.
