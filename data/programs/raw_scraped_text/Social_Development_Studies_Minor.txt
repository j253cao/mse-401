Social Development Studies Minor
Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program. 
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative minor average of 65.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 4.0 units of SDS and approved courses.

Students may only complete one course from any cross-listed set.
Transfer credits with direct equivalencies to any specific course listed above and unspecific transfer credits (e.g., SDS2XX) with the SDS subject code at the 200-level and above may be counted towards this plan; common degree requirements (e.g., residency, 50% rule) must still be met.
