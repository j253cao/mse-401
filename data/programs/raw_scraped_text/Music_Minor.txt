Music Minor
Music Minor
Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program. 
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative minor average of 65.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 4.0 units of MUSIC courses.
Participate in at least two terms of Music Ensemble: MUSIC116, MUSIC117, MUSIC216, MUSIC217, MUSIC316, MUSIC317, MUSIC416, MUSIC417; which may not be counted towards the total 4.0 units.
Course Requirements
Required Courses
keyboard_arrow_up
Complete all of the following
Complete all the following: 
MUSIC110 - How Music Matters (0.50)
MUSIC270 - Music Theory 1 (0.50)
Complete 1 of the following: 
MUSIC222 - Conducting 1 (0.50)
MUSIC233 - Musical Rhythms of the World (0.50)
Complete 2.5 additional units of MUSIC courses 
The following cannot be used towards this academic plan: 
MUSIC111 - Fundamentals of Music Theory (0.50)
Additional Constraints
Students electing to take Music studio must arrange for an audition before the Music faculty. Normally, a level of performance equivalent to about Grade Eight standing at the Royal Conservatory of Music of Toronto is expected for admission to Music studio. Non-western musical instruments will also be considered at the discretion of the Department.
Students pursuing a Music Minor may take a maximum of three terms of Music studio.
