Psychology Minor
Psychology Minor
Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program. 
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative minor average of 65.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 4.0 units in PSYCH courses.
Course Requirements
Required Courses
keyboard_arrow_up

Complete 1 of the following: 
PSYCH101 - Introductory Psychology (0.50)
PSYCH101R - Introductory Psychology (0.50)
Complete 1 of the following: 
PSYCH207 - Cognitive Processes (0.50)
PSYCH261 - Physiological Psychology (0.50)
Complete 2 of the following: 
PSYCH207 - Cognitive Processes (0.50)
PSYCH211 - Developmental Psychology (0.50)
PSYCH238 - Organizational Psychology (0.50)
PSYCH253 - Social Psychology (0.50)
PSYCH253R - Social Psychology (0.50)
PSYCH257 - Psychopathology (0.50)
PSYCH257R - Psychopathology (0.50)
PSYCH261 - Physiological Psychology (0.50)
Complete 1.0 unit of PSYCH courses at the 300-level or above
Complete 1.0 additional unit of PSYCH courses
Additional Constraints
Students may only complete one course from any cross-listed set.
No one course may fulfil more than one requirement within the Minor.
