Environment, Resources and Sustainability Minor
Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program. 
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative minor average of 65.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 5.0 units of ERS and ENVS courses.
A maximum of 2.0 units of ENVS courses can be used towards this Minor.
