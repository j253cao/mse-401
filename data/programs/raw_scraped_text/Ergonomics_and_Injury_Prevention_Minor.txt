Ergonomics and Injury Prevention Minor
Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program. 
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative minor average of 60.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 5.0 units.

Additional Constraints
No one course may fulfil more than one requirement within the Minor.
Only one of KIN433 and KIN472 can be used towards this Minor.
To use KIN431, KIN432, KIN433, and KIN472 towards this Minor, the topic must be in ergonomics and injury prevention and be approved in advance by the course supervisor and Associate Chair, Undergraduate Studies.
Courses obtained on a Letter of Permission or in transfer credit must be equivalent to courses listed in the course requirements.
