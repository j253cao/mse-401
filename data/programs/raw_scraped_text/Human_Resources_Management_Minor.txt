Human Resources Management Minor
Human Resources Management Minor
Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program. 
Declaration Requirements
HRM200.
Minimum Human Resources Management plan average of 70.0%.
Current satisfactory standing in the other academic plans.

Before declaring this academic plan, see invalid credential combinations.

Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative minor average of 70.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 4.0 units.
