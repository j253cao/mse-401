Russian and East European Studies Minor
Russian and East European Studies Minor
Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program. 
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative minor average of 65.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 4.0 units.

Notes
In addition to basic language competency in both Russian and Croatian, the Russian and East European Studies (REES) Minor includes courses in culture, literature, film, and linguistics, taught in English.
Visit the Department of Germanic and Slavic Studies website for further information.
