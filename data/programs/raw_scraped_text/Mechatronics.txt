Mechatronics Option
This option is available for students in the following degrees
Bachelor of Applied Science
Bachelor of Software Engineering
Declaration Requirements
Before requesting admission to this academic plan, see invalid credential combinations.
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative option average of 60.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 5.0 units.

Additional Constraints
The two-term design project/workshop requirement must have a mechatronics theme which must be approved by the faculty Mechatronics Option co-ordinator. Students must also meet the project/workshop requirements of their home department.
Notes
Students are encouraged to contact the Mechatronics Option co-ordinator if they have difficulty enrolling in a course.
Exceptions to the requirements and electives listed above require approval of the Option co-ordinator.
