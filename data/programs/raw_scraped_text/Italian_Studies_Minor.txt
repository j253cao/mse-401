Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program.

A minimum cumulative minor average of 65.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 4.0 units.

Additional Constraints
Up to 1.0 unit of courses from the list of Approved Courses can be used towards the ITALST unit requirement.
Using approved courses towards the requirements:
Only one of LAT101 or LAT102 may be used.
HIST351 may be used only with permission of Italian Studies.
CLAS390 may be used only when offered in Italy.
