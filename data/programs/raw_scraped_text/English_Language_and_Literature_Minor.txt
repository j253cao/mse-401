English Language and Literature Minor
English Language and Literature Minor
Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program. 
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative minor average of 65.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 4.0 units in ENGL courses.
Course Requirements
Required Courses
keyboard_arrow_up

Complete all the following: 
ENGL251 - Literary Theory and Criticism (0.50)
Complete 2 of the following: 
ENGL200A - English Literatures 1 (0.50)
ENGL200B - English Literatures 2 (0.50)
ENGL200C - English Literatures 3 (0.50)
Complete 0.5 additional unit of ENGL courses at the 300-level or above
Complete 2.0 additional units of ENGL courses
Complete no more than 2 from the following: 
COMMST323 - Speech Writing (0.50)
ENGL210E - Genres of Technical Communication (0.50)
ENGL210F - Genres of Business Communication (0.50)
ENGL210G - Genres of Fundraising Communication (0.50)
ENGL210H - Arts Writing (0.50)
ENGL210I - Legal Writing (0.50)
ENGL309E - Speech Writing (0.50)
LS291 - Legal Writing (0.50)
Additional Constraints
Students may only complete one course from any cross-listed set.
Students may use no more than two 100-level courses.
Courses transferred from other institutions without a specific course designation (e.g., ENGL1XX) may only be counted towards the English electives.
