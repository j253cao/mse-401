Knowledge Integration Minor
Knowledge Integration Minor
Special Notice

The Faculty of Environment is undertaking a comprehensive review of the Knowledge Integration Minor undergraduate plan. As a result, there is a pause on declaring this plan as an additional credential. Students currently in this plan should contact the academic advisor for guidance on completion of this minor.

Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program. 
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative minor average of 65.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 4.0 units.
