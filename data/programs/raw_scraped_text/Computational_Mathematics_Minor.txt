Additional Constraints
In List 1:
Choices must be in at least two different subject codes (AMATH, CO, CS, PMATH, STAT).
Only one of CS431 or CS451 may be taken.
BIOL382 counts as an AMATH course for the purpose of the "at least two different subject codes" requirement.
For Data Science majors, a maximum of five CS or STAT courses (or their cross-listing) can be used to fulfil the requirements of the Minor.
For Computer Science majors, a maximum of four CS courses (or their cross-listing) can be used to fulfil the requirements of the Minor.
Students may only complete one course from any cross-listed set.
