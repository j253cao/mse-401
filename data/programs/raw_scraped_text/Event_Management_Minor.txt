Additional Constraints
Students may only complete one course from any cross-listed set.
Normally, a maximum of two courses (1.0 unit) obtained on a Letter of Permission or in transfer credit may be applied towards this Minor. These courses must be equivalent to the course requirements as assessed by the school/department offering the replaced course.
