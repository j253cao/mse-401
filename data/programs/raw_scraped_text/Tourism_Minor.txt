Tourism Minor
Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program. 
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative minor average of 65.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 4.0 units:
2.0 units of required courses.
2.0 units of approved courses.

Additional Constraints
Students may only complete one course from any cross-listed set.
To use REC475 towards this Minor, the topic must be "Tourism/Commercial Recreation".
Topics for REC417A and REC471B or GEOG490A and GEOG490B, if related to the Minor, may be approved by the Department of Recreation and Leisure Studies, Associate Chair, Undergraduate Studies, may be counted as tourism approved courses.
