Environmental Engineering Option
This option is available for students in the following degrees
Bachelor of Applied Science
Bachelor of Software Engineering
Declaration Requirements
Before declaring this academic plan, see invalid credential combinations.
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative option average of 60.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 3.5 units:
0.5 unit from List 1.
0.5 unit from List 2.
1.0 unit from List 3.
0.5 unit from List 4.
0.5 unit from List 2, List 3, or List 4.
