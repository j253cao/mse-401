Addictions, Mental Health, and Policy Minor
Addictions, Mental Health, and Policy Minor
Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program. 
Declaration Requirements
Students must be in level at least 2A to declare the Minor.
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative minor average of 67.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 5.0 units.

Additional Constraints
Students may only complete one course from any cross-listed set.
All three PSYCH honours thesis courses must be completed (PSYCH499A, PSYCH 499B, PSYCH499C).
The thesis, independent study, and seminar courses must be a topic on the neuroscience, biology, psychosocial, or policy aspects of addictions or mental health that is approved by the School of Public Health Sciences Associate Director, Undergraduate Studies.
