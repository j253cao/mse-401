Spanish Minor
Spanish Minor
Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program. 
Declaration Requirements
Before declaring this academic plan, see invalid credential combinations.
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative minor average of 65.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 4.0 units in SPAN courses.
Additional Constraints
By agreement, students at the University of Waterloo and Wilfrid Laurier University are allowed to take courses in Spanish at either university.
With the permission of the Department of Spanish and Latin American Studies, students may study at a university in Spain or Latin America.
Notes
Many Spanish (SPAN) courses are taught in English and require no knowledge of Spanish; these courses are so identified by their course descriptions.
Consult the undergraduate officer in Spanish and Latin American Studies for the Spanish Minor.
Visit the Department of Spanish and Latin American Studies website for further information.
