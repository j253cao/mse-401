Actuarial Science Minor
Actuarial Science Minor
Declaration Requirements
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program in the Faculty of Mathematics.
To declare the Actuarial Science Minor, students are required to have:

Completed MTHEL131 with a minimum grade of 60.0%; or, for Business Administration and Mathematics Double degree students, a minimum grade of C- in BUS121W.
A minimum major average (MAV) of 70.0%; or, if a MAV does not yet exist, a minimum cumulative average of 70.0% on at least 10 courses, and no failed courses (i.e., no grades below 50.0%, or DNWs, or WFs).
Before declaring this academic plan, see invalid credential combinations.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 5.0 units.

BUS393W for ACTSC372.
STAT334 is not an acceptable substitute for STAT330 or STAT333.
