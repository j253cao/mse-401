Urban Studies Minor
Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program. 
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative minor average of 65.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 4.5 units:
Complete 2.0 units of required courses.
Complete 2.5 units of courses from one theme described below.
