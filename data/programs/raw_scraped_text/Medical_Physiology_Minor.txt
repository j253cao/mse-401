Additional Constraints
Students may only complete one course from any cross-listed set.
Courses obtained on a Letter of Permission or in transfer credit must be equivalent to courses listed in the course requirements.
A failed second attempt of a required course will result in removal from the Minor; no third attempts are allowed.
Notes
Students are expected to have already taken BIOL130, BIOL273, and PSYCH101/PSYCH101R, which are prerequisites for one or more of the listed required courses.
