Additional Constraints
Students may only complete one course from any cross-listed set.
To complete the approved courses requirement of 2.0 units from List 2 (or additional courses from List 1), no more than two courses can be taken from the subject subject code.
Relevant advanced seminars and topics courses in the participating academic units, approved by the cognitive science academic advisor or the Department of Psychology undergraduate officer, may be used towards List 2.
