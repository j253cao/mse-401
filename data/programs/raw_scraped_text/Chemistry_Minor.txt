Chemistry Minor
Chemistry Minor
Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program. 
Declaration Requirements
Before declaring this academic plan, see invalid credential combinations.
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative average of 60.0%: all lecture courses.
A minimum cumulative average of 60.0%: all laboratory courses.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 5.0 units.
Course Requirements
Required Courses
keyboard_arrow_up
Complete all of the following
Complete 1 of the following: 
CHEM120 - General Chemistry 1 (0.50)
CHEM121 - Physical and Chemical Properties of Matter (0.50)
Complete 1 of the following: 
CHEM120L - General Chemistry Laboratory 1 (0.25)
CHEM121L - Chemical Reaction Laboratory 1 (0.25)
Complete 1 of the following: 
CHEM123 - General Chemistry 2 (0.50)
CHEM125 - Chemical Reactions, Equilibria and Kinetics (0.50)
Complete 1 of the following: 
CHEM123L - General Chemistry Laboratory 2 (0.25)
CHEM125L - Chemical Reaction Laboratory 2 (0.25)
Complete 3.0 units of CHEM lecture courses, with a minimum of 1.0 unit at the 300-level or above.
Complete 0.5 unit of CHEM laboratory courses, at the 200-level or above.
Additional Constraints
A maximum of two failures in CHEM courses is allowed.
The courses counted towards the 3.0 CHEM lecture units requirement must span a minimum of three theme areas. For 200-level or above CHEM courses, the middle digit in the course number designates the theme area:
X0X general interest
X1X inorganic/materials chemistry
X2X analytical chemistry
X3X biological chemistry/biochemistry
X4X computational/theoretical chemistry
X5X physical chemistry
X6X organic chemistry
X7X polymer chemistry
X8X medicinal chemistry
X9X individualized courses (research projects, etc.)
