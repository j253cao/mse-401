Economic Theory Minor
Economic Theory Minor
Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program. 
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative minor average of 65.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 4.0 units.
Course Requirements
Required Courses
keyboard_arrow_up
Complete all the following: 
ECON101 - Introduction to Microeconomics (0.50)
ECON102 - Introduction to Macroeconomics (0.50)
ECON211 - Introduction to Mathematical Economics (0.50)
ECON221 - Statistics for Economists (0.50)
ECON290 - Models of Choice in Competitive Markets (0.50)
ECON391 - Equilibrium in Market Economies (0.50)
ECON392 - Strategic Situations and Welfare Economics (0.50)
ECON393 - Market Failures (0.50)
Additional Constraints
Students in the Faculty of Mathematics who cannot take ECON211 or ECON221 will have to replace those courses with electives in Economics of their choice.
