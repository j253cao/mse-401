Computer Engineering Option
This option is available for students in the following degrees
Bachelor of Applied Science
Bachelor of Architectural Studies
Bachelor of Software Engineering
Declaration Requirements
Before declaring this academic plan, see invalid credential combinations.
Students may not declare this Option until they have completed a List 1 course and a List 2 course. Students must have a minimum average of 75.0% calculated across these two courses in order to declare this Option.
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative option average of 75.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 4.0 units:
1.0 unit of required courses.
3.0 units of approved courses:

0.5 unit from List 1.
0.5 unit from List 2.
1.5 units from List 3.
0.5 unit from List 4.

Additional Constraints
Five of the courses must be considered as electives in the student's major academic plan. For the purposes of this Option, a course that a student could choose to graduate without will be considered elective.
Students who take CS136 or CS146 are also required to enrol in CS136L. However, passing CS136L is not a requirement for the Option and the course may be coded as NRNA.
Exceptions to the courses listed here may be approved by the Option co-ordinator.
