Neuroscience Minor
Neuroscience Minor
Student Audience
This credential is open to students enrolled in any degree program. 
Declaration Requirements
Before declaring this academic plan, see invalid credential combinations.
Students in the Neuroscience Minor are expected to have already taken BIOL130, BIOL273, and PSYCH101, which are prerequisites for one or more of the courses listed.
Minimum Average(s) Required
A minimum cumulative minor average of 60.0%.
Graduation Requirements
Complete a total of 5.0 units:
2.5 units of required courses.
2.5 units of approved courses (see Additional Constraints).

Additional Constraints
For the Required Courses list, a maximum of 1.5 units can be from PSYCH.
For the approved courses requirement, a maximum of 1.5 units can be from the same subject.
List 2: Courses must be a topic on neuroscience or related area of study that is approved by the Associate Chair/Director, Undergraduate Studies, for the respective department/school in charge of the course listed. Independent project, senior essay, or honours thesis courses from majors not listed (e.g., Sociology) must be a topic on neuroscience or related area of study that is approved by the Department of Kinesiology and Health Sciences Associate Chair, Undergraduate Studies.
Courses obtained on a Letter of Permission or in transfer credit must be equivalent to courses listed in the course requirements.
